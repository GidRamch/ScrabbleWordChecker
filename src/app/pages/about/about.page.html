Check Scrabble words offline! (based on Collins 2019 Scrabble Dictionary). Built with the Ionic Framework (Version 5).

How to use?

Any player may question the validity of words formed by Player A on Player A's most recent turn.
The challenging player must say 'challenge', after which the words formed by Player A will be checked against the dictionary. (Check Words page).
If any word is not in the dictionary, Player A must remove their tiles from the board and lose their turn. If all words are in the dictionary, the challenging player loses their turn.
Challenging cannot be done if Player A has already drawn and looked at their replacement tiles.
